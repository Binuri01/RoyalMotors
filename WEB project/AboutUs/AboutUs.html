About Us

Royal Motors (PVT) Ltd was established in 1982. Throughout the 40 years of excellence we have gained a sound level of experience and knowledge to serve our customers to our best extent. As a result of spreading our arms across the country, we have 3 branches at Nugegoda, Galle and Matara

Our Vision is to be the best motor sales company in Sri Lanka by maintaining the known status in the transport industry.

Our Mission is to develop life long relationships with our customers by providing their required services.
